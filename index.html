Theme

Auto-Lock Timer

Change Password
Warning: Re-encrypts all files. May take a while for large vaults.
Two-Factor Authentication
Uses TOTP (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password…). The secret is stored encrypted inside your vault.
Vault Signing
Signs the vault on every save using ECDSA P-256. Detects tampering before you even enter your password.
My Keypairs
Your P-256 keypairs. Share a public key with anyone who wants to send you a vault. Keep the private key secret — it never leaves this vault.
Share With (Recipients)
Recipients can open this vault with their own private key — no password required. Paste or load their public key file. Changes take effect on next save.
Drag & Drop
When enabled, you can drag files from your desktop into the Add Files zone. Off by default — only enable on trusted devices.
